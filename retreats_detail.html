Program Summary

Summary:

A 8-days transformative opportunity to heal and rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit. Embrace the experience fully and be open to the healing energy and emotions that may come your way. Together and with professional experts we will teach you all the important details about how to feel the subtle energy during your yoga practice and make a link with other teachings as pranayama, Tai chi and Qigong.

We will also share yogic basics principles of having a healthy and real life style. Philosophy, theology and fun are also on the agenda. Together we will visit important spiritual places for energy healing.

Location:

Restore and relax on the beauty of the caribbean sea in Mexico's magic island. All this is done while keeping up with a spiritual-yoga-meditation-centered retreat schedule. I will teach you how to feel and manipulate the energy inside and outside your body in order for this energy to serve you for your self-healing life journey. We will mix millennial teachings such as Tai Chi, Qi Gong, and Yoga.

Cozumel, Quintana Roo, Mexico

Program Details

Every day, we will start with a pranayama cleansing exercise followed by meditation and a relaxing morning yoga. Then we will have breakfast and get ready for the day, whereas we have some theory lecture, philosophy of yoga or a day visit to a Spiritual or Holly place.

Later in the day, we will have lunch and continue our activities, whether it is a yoga session, free time, or personal therapy. Some days, we also have yoga in the evening before dinner.

Every evening, we will have special nights where we will do inner work, share a cacao ceremony, make manifestation rituals, nidra yoga, affirmations, and meditation.

Every day, the program is different, but we will be doing yoga, pranayama, and meditation every day at least once time per day.
